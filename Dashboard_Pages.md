# 📊 Dashboard Pages Overview

## Page 1: Overview Dashboard
- Displays key KPIs such as Avg Temperature, Max Temperature, and Total Rainfall
- Shows year-wise trends for temperature and rainfall
- Provides quick insights using slicers (Year, Month)

## Page 2: Seasonal & Monthly Analysis
- Analyzes monthly temperature and rainfall patterns
- Includes a Month × Year temperature heatmap
- Uses KPI strip and custom tooltip for detailed insights

## Page 3: Extreme Weather Analysis
- Identifies extreme hot days (>40°C)
- Tracks heavy rainfall days (>50 mm)
- Highlights climate risk and anomaly trends
